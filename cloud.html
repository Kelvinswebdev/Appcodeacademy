Cloud Computing

Cloud technologies are enabling organisations to transform rapidly, creating a high demand for cloud-savvy employees. As more and more companies adopt cloud services, there’s an immense drive to hire people with cloud computing skills, which LinkedIn recognised as the most in-demand hard skills in the tech market.

The Appcode Cloud Practitioner Programme will provide you with a solid understanding of AWS Cloud concepts and services – without any prior IT experience needed. Prepare for in-demand cloud jobs with this globally-recognised certification and become an expert in a rapidly growing industry, making sure you stay ahead of the curve in Cloud innovation.

In partnership with Amazon Web Services (AWS), the market leader among the world’s biggest cloud services, the AWS Cloud Practitioner programme will prepare you for a career with top international companies that use cloud-based technologies, such as Samsung, BMW, Sony, Unilever, Johnson & Johnson, Airbnb, Siemens, Adobe, Netflix, Pfizer, and many more. Our Cloud Practitioner Programme is inclusive of the highly rated 3-month ALX Foundations module, preparing learners with the sought-after professional skills for today’s digital workforce.

If you want to start a successful career in IT, now is the time to gain expertise and training in cloud computing, with Appcode and AWS.
